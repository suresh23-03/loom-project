OUR Vision & Mission

Vision & Mission

Vision: To catalyze the transformation of the Dharmapuri powerloom sector into a dynamic hub of innovation, efficiency, and sustainable growth, empowering micro-enterprises to thrive in the global textile
Mission: Our mission is to drive the advancement of the Dharmapuri powerloom sector by providing cutting-edge infrastructure, technology, and support services through our Common Facility Centre. We are dedicated to fostering a culture of collaboration, excellence, and inclusivity, thereby enabling micro-enterprises to achieve unparalleled levels of productivity, competitiveness, and success. Through strategic partnerships and relentless commitment to quality and innovation, we aim to propel the powerloom industry in Tamil Nadu towards a prosperous and sustainable future.
